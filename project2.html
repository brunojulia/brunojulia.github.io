Josephson effect in Bose-Einstein condensates

During the last five years we have studied a number of aspects of the physics of ultracold atomic clouds trapped in double-well potentials. In particular, we have studied spinor condensates, binary mixtures, formation of correlated ground states, temperature effects, etc.
