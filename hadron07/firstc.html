Workshop for the HADRONTH network

Dear friends of the HADRONTH network, the Barcelona node has the task to
organize a Workshop for the collaboration. The dates will be

October 1-5 , 2007

The place will be Barcelona, well known mediterranean capital.

In order to encourage participation in this last collaboration meeting 
of the HADRONTH network activity of the HADRON I3 project, we plan to 
reflect in the program all the subjects covered by the nodes, in general 
lines: hadron properties in the vacuum and in the medium, and hadron 
chiral dynamics.

Thus, the duration of the workshop will be slightly longer than usual. 
The program will start on monday 1st in the afternoon and will end on 
friday 5th before lunch to facilitate the organization of your trip. 
Nevertheless we recommend the participants to stay over the weekend to 
visit the city, which in that period of the year should be very pleasant.

THERE WILL BE NO CONFERENCE FEE.

TO ENCOURAGE THE PARTICIPATION OF THE YOUNGER PEOPLE WE PLAN TO COVER THE 
LOCAL EXPENSES OF THE GRADUATE STUDENTS.
